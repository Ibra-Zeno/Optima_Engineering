Excelling in Project Management

Master the art of Project Planning with Optima's comprehensive online courses, tailored to harness the full potential of the powerful software 'PowerProject'. Our goal is to empower you with the skills and knowledge to excel in project management, and our thorough online courses are designed to do just that.

Navigating the Learning Curve

With a range of difficulty levels available, including beginner, intermediate, and advanced, participants can select the course that best aligns with their existing knowledge and experience. Each level is carefully crafted to address specific learning objectives while emphasising the importance of efficient project planning methodologies:

  • Level 1 - Beginners Project Management:
    The beginner level course is ideal for those who are new to project planning or are looking to refresh their foundational skills.
    Participants will gain a solid understanding of project management principles and learn how to leverage the 'PowerProject' software to create project plans, define tasks and dependencies, allocate resources, and generate basic reports.
  • Level 2 - Intermediate Project Management:
    The intermediate level course is designed for project managers with a moderate level of experience seeking to enhance their proficiency in project planning and management.
    Participants will learn how to optimise resource utilisation, manage complex scheduling scenarios, manage project risks, and effectively track project progress. Practical case studies and real-world examples will further reinforce the learning experience.
  • Level 3 - Advanced Project Management:
    The advanced level course caters to seasoned project managers and professionals who are already proficient in project planning and management techniques.
    This course focuses on advanced project planning strategies and explores innovative ways to utilise the 'PowerProject' software. Participants will delve into topics such as agile project management, stakeholder engagement, critical path analysis, and portfolio management.
